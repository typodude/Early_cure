About

The system analyzes palm images to identify potential health risks. By examining palm lines and patterns, it predicts conditions such as Hypertension, Cardiovascular Diseases, Down Syndrome, Neurological Disorders, and more.

Leveraging deep learning models, the system provides accurate predictions and actionable insights, enabling users to take preventive steps for improved health. The user interface is designed to ensure smooth navigation and an effective health assessment experience.

Hypertension & Cardiovascular Disease

Hypertension is a condition where blood pressure is consistently too high. It is a major risk factor for cardiovascular diseases.

Causes & Risk Factors:

  • Obesity
  • High salt intake
  • Stress and anxiety
  • Genetic predisposition

Down Syndrome - Heart Defects & Immune Issues

Down Syndrome is a genetic condition that affects cognitive ability and growth, often accompanied by heart defects and immune issues.

Causes & Risk Factors:

  • Genetic condition (extra copy of chromosome 21)
  • Maternal age over 35
  • Family history of Down Syndrome

Neurological Disorders - Autism & Schizophrenia

Neurological disorders such as Autism and Schizophrenia affect brain function, behavior, and cognitive abilities.

Causes & Risk Factors:

  • Genetic mutations
  • Brain injuries or trauma
  • Environmental toxins during pregnancy

Scleroderma - Tissue Disorder

Scleroderma is a chronic autoimmune disease that results in hardening of the skin and connective tissues.

Causes & Risk Factors:

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Immune system dysfunction
  • Environmental factors

Healthy Lifestyle Insights

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle includes regular exercise, balanced nutrition, and mental well-being for overall vitality.

Causes & Risk Factors for Poor Health:

  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Poor eating habits
  • Inadequate sleep
